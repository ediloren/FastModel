Launch FastHenry2

To launch FastHenry2 on your input file, simply click the FastHenry2 icon on the toolbar (a white button with a red inductance symbol on it).

Note: you cannot launch FastHenry2 on an unsaved file. If your file contains recent unsaved modifications, it will be automatically saved. However, if the file is unnamed (you began from a new empty window) you will be prompted to save it with a name.
