Launch FastCap2

To launch FastCap2 on your input file, simply click the FastCap2 icon on the toolbar (the white button with a blue capacitor symbol on it).

Note: you cannot launch FastCap2 on unsaved files. If your files contain recent unsaved modifications, they will be automatically saved. However, if any of the files is unnamed (you began from a new empty window) you will be prompted to saved it with a name.
