Launch FasterCap

To launch FasterCap on your input file, simply click the FasterCap icon on the toolbar (the yellow button with a blue capacitor symbol on it).

Note: you cannot launch FasterCap on unsaved files. If your files contain recent unsaved modifications, they will be automatically saved. However, if any of the files is unnamed (you began from a new empty window) you will be prompted to saved it with a name.
